# 4 Weeks to Independence

A step-by-step program to learn your smart glasses. One small task per day, 7 days per week, 4 weeks total. Check off each day as you go.

{% set legend = "Choose your phone" %}{% include "components/phone-toggle.njk" %}

## Week 1 — Setup & First Words {#week-1}

<details>
<summary>Day 1 — Unbox & Charge</summary>
<div>

Open the box, charge glasses and case, try them on for fit. Learn where the controls are.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w1d1"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 2 — Pair with Phone</summary>
<div>

Install Meta View app, pair your glasses, run firmware update.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w1d2"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 3 — "Hey Meta, what do you see?"</summary>
<div>

Your first AI interaction. Say it 5 times in different rooms. See what Meta describes.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w1d3"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 4 — Phone Accessibility</summary>
<div>

<div class="iphone-only">

Set up VoiceOver, Announce Notifications, and Siri on your iPhone.

</div>
<div class="android-only">

Set up TalkBack, notification reading, and Google Assistant on your Android phone.

</div>

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w1d4"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 5 — Touchpad & Gestures</summary>
<div>

Practice: tap, double-tap, swipe forward, swipe back, tap-and-hold.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w1d5"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 6 — Make a Call + Install Core Apps</summary>
<div>

Call a friend: "Hey Meta, call [name]." Then install the 8 essential apps from the [Apps page](/en/apps/).

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w1d6"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 7 — Rest & Review</summary>
<div>

What worked? What was confusing? Write it down. Rest before Week 2.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w1d7"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

## Week 2 — Seeing with AI {#week-2}

<details>
<summary>Day 1 — Scene Description</summary>
<div>

"Hey Meta, what's in front of me?" — practice indoors.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w2d1"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 2 — Reading Text</summary>
<div>

Read a menu, a letter, a medicine label.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w2d2"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 3 — Object Identification</summary>
<div>

"What is this?" — products, food, household items.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w2d3"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 4 — Color Identification</summary>
<div>

"What color is this shirt?" — practice with clothing, food, objects.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w2d4"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 5 — Follow-up Questions</summary>
<div>

Multi-turn: "What do you see?" → "Is there a price tag?" → "How much?"

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w2d5"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 6 — Outdoors</summary>
<div>

Walk outside. Describe buildings, signs, surroundings. Practice at crossings.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w2d6"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 7 — Rest & Review</summary>
<div>

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w2d7"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

## Week 3 — Your Phone Assistant {#week-3}

<details>
<summary>Day 1 — Siri / Google Basics</summary>
<div>

<div class="iphone-only">"Hey Siri" — weather, time, reminders, timers.</div>
<div class="android-only">"Hey Google" — weather, time, reminders, timers.</div>

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w3d1"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 2 — Navigation</summary>
<div>

"Navigate to [address]" with Google Maps. Walk a familiar route first.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w3d2"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 3 — Messages</summary>
<div>

Send a WhatsApp via Meta AI. Send an SMS via Siri/Google.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w3d3"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 4 — Shortcuts & Routines</summary>
<div>

<div class="iphone-only">Set up 3 Siri Shortcuts: morning briefing, navigate home, safety check.</div>
<div class="android-only">Set up Google Routines: morning briefing, navigate home.</div>

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w3d4"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 5 — Music & Media</summary>
<div>

"Hey Meta, play [playlist]" via Spotify Tap.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w3d5"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 6 — Smart Home (optional)</summary>
<div>

Control lights, thermostat by voice. Skip this if you don't have smart home devices.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w3d6"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>

<details>
<summary>Day 7 — Two Wake Words</summary>
<div>

Practice switching between "Hey Meta" and "Hey Siri" / "Hey Google" naturally.

<label class="progress-check"><input type="checkbox" id="w3d7"> Done</label>

</div>
</details>
