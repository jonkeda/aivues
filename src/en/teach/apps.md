# Essential Apps

There are seven apps that make the glasses more useful. Your helper can install them.

Meta AI is the companion app for the glasses. You need it for pairing and settings. It is required.

Be My Eyes connects you with a volunteer who can see through your glasses camera. Use it when the AI can't answer your question. A real person helps you.

WhatsApp lets you send and receive messages through the glasses. Say Hey Meta, send a message to someone on WhatsApp.

Envision AI reads text, describes scenes, and finds objects. It works with the phone camera and also with Envision's own smart glasses.

Ally is an accessible AI assistant made by Envision. You can voice chat, get image descriptions, and search the web. It is designed for blind and low vision users.

ScribeMe is an AI scanner and live visual assistant. It reads documents, describes surroundings in real time, and answers questions about what it sees.

Spotify plays music through the glasses. Say Hey Meta, play and then a song or playlist.

Want to try one? Say: Hey Meta, play some music. That uses Spotify through the glasses.
