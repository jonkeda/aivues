# Training Overview

AiVues has a 28-lesson training program split into 4 guides. You can go at your own pace.

Guide 1 is Setup and First Words. You unbox, charge, pair, and speak your first commands. By the end you'll be talking to Meta AI and making calls.

Guide 2 is Seeing with AI. You practice describing scenes, reading text, identifying objects, and recognizing colors. You learn follow-up questions and outdoor use.

Guide 3 is Your Phone Assistant. You learn Siri or Google Assistant for navigation, messages, timers, shortcuts, and music. You practice using both wake words together.

Guide 4 is Apps and Independence. You try Be My Eyes, Envision AI, Ally, and ScribeMe. You put together a full daily routine and graduate.

To start with a specific topic, just tell me what you want to learn. Say: Hey Meta, teach me about reading. Or: Hey Meta, teach me the controls. Or: Hey Meta, teach me about apps.

For the full step-by-step program with checkboxes, ask your helper to visit aiviews.nl/en/training.
