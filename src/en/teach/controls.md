# Glasses Controls

You have two controls on your glasses. Both are on the right side.

The first is the touchpad. It's a smooth strip along the right temple. You can feel it with your finger.

Tap the touchpad once to pause or play music. Tap it twice quickly to skip to the next song. Swipe your finger from front to back along the touchpad to turn the volume up. Swipe from back to front to turn it down. Press and hold the touchpad to activate Meta AI without saying Hey Meta.

The second control is the capture button. It's a small button on top of the right temple, toward the front. Press it once to take a photo. Hold it down to record a video.

There is also a privacy light on the outside of the frame, near the hinge on the right side. It turns on whenever the camera is active. You cannot turn it off. It's designed so people around you know the camera is in use.

To check your battery, say Hey Meta, battery. A full charge lasts about 4 to 6 hours. The charging case holds about 8 more full charges.

Want to try something? Say: Hey Meta, play some music. Then practice the tap, double-tap, and swipe gestures.
