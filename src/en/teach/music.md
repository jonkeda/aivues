# Music

Say Hey Meta, play, and then a song name, an artist, or a playlist. Music plays through the speakers in the glasses temples, near your ears.

This works with Spotify. You need the Spotify app installed and signed in.

To control playback, use the touchpad on the right side of the frame. Tap once to pause. Tap again to play. Tap twice to skip to the next song. Swipe front to back for volume up. Swipe back to front for volume down.

You can also use voice. Hey Meta, pause. Hey Meta, next. Hey Meta, volume up.

Try these: Hey Meta, play some jazz. Hey Meta, play my Discover Weekly. Hey Meta, play songs by Adele.

Music and podcasts are great for everyday use. The glasses speakers are open-ear, meaning you can still hear your surroundings while listening, which is important for safety.

Want to try? Say: Hey Meta, play some music. Then practice the touchpad gestures.
